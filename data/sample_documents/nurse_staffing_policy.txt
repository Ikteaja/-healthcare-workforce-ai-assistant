Nurse Staffing Policy

Purpose: Establish safe, acuity-aware staffing practices for inpatient nursing teams.

Policy: Unit leaders review census, patient acuity, isolation needs, and available competencies at the start of each shift. Staffing coordinators escalate gaps to the house supervisor when nurse-to-patient targets cannot be met.

Procedure: Charge nurses update the staffing board every four hours, document float pool requests, and notify human resources of recurring vacancies that affect coverage. Leaders prioritize patient safety, required breaks, and continuity of care when reassigning team members.
