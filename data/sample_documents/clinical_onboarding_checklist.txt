Clinical Workforce Onboarding Checklist

1. Verify license, certification, background screening, immunization status, and role-specific competencies before the first scheduled shift.
2. Assign each new hire a preceptor and document orientation milestones in the learning management system.
3. Confirm completion of electronic health record training, emergency preparedness training, infection prevention education, and workplace violence prevention modules.
4. Review escalation paths for staffing concerns, patient safety events, and compliance questions.
